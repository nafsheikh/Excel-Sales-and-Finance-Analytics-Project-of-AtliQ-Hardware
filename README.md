## Sales & Finance Analytics Using Excel

Welcome to this end-to-end Excel analytics project. This work demonstrates how raw business data can be transformed into structured insights that support smarter decision-making in both sales and finance functions.

The project focuses on building practical reports that business teams can actually use to monitor performance, identify opportunities, and maintain financial control.

 ## Project Purpose

The primary objective of this project was to:
Convert unstructured data into meaningful analysis
Evaluate sales performance across customers and markets
Examine financial health using profit and loss reporting
Build clear, decision-ready dashboards in Excel

## Sales Analytics

Customer Performance Evaluation
Analyzed customer-level sales to understand contribution patterns, growth behavior, and revenue concentration.

Sales vs Market Comparison
Performed a comparative study between internal sales results and overall market performance to uncover gaps and opportunities.



## Finance Analytics

Profit & Loss by Fiscal Year
Prepared structured P&L statements to track yearly financial movement and profitability trends.

Profitability by Market
Evaluated how different markets contribute to overall financial performance.
